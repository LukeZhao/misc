Open this page on two chrome browsers. From one of it click the 'get tokens' button to get twilio video tokens. Two tokens will be generated in addition to two ids. copy the clinician token to one browser, and the patient token to the other (where it says 'Token to paste here'), and click 'Start video' button (make sure you have webcam connected) for both. Then paste the patient id to the session where the clinician token is used, and then click 'invite' button, this will invite the patient to join the video session. If everythign goes well, a video conversation should be started.
